Fullmetal Alchemist

Meet the legendary characters

Edward Elric

The Fullmetal Alchemist

The youngest State Alchemist in history. He's on a journey to find the Philosopher's Stone to restore his and his brother's bodies.

Alphonse Elric

The Soul in Armor

Edward's younger brother. After a failed transmutation, his soul was bound to a giant suit of armor. He is kind and protective.

Roy Mustang

The Flame Alchemist

A high-ranking State Alchemist and military officer. He wields powerful fire alchemy and aims to become the next Führer.

Winry Rockbell

Automail Mechanic

A talented mechanic and the Elric brothers' childhood friend. She provides constant support with her expert automail repairs.
